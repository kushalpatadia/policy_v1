Santander Contractor Mortgages

Santander are a great lender with some really good interest rates.

They are very busy so expect them to take a while for you to make an appointment to see the Santander Mortgage Advisor for a Santander Contractor Mortgage, which is not the same as offered by other lenders, Santander tend to require a contract to be for at least 2 years in the future, basically really onerous conditions to meet policy.

Santander have some great products, but they remain a non-contractor friendly lender They often tell their customers that they can help with a contractor mortgage, but in truth this is something that they cannot do.

The individual relationship with the high street bank manager has gone and the bank manager is in no position to make a decision based upon merit at his discretion. If the computer says no then the answer is a generally no, not just for Santander, but for most lenders.

So, to cap it off, Santander are not a contractor friendly lender but remain a good lender who do things many others do not.
